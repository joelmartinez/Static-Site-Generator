Introducing Viewer for Khan Academy (Windows 8)

By Joel Martinez on 09/27/2012



After much hard work, I'm happy to announce the release of a project I've been working on, Viewer for Khan Academy on the Windows Store (windows 8). The source code can be found on GitHub.

I really respect and appreciate what they're doing over at the Khan Academy. With two small children of my own, anything that I can do to bring awareness to their great content and help enrich tomorrow's generation (and even today's) is time well spent in my book.

Huge shoutout to the folks over at Channel 9, who have produced a great set of tutorials and videos on the development of v1 of the app. The tutorials are written from the perspective of porting the Windows Phone 7 version of the app to Windows 8. Many thanks and props to Rick, Brian, Clint, and Dan. And of course, all of this wouldn't be possible without the effort put forth by the Khan Academy Team.

Thanks!
