Social Media Diet

By Joel Martinez on 07/07/2020

This has been a long time coming, but I'd never actually gathered up the ... courage isn't the right word ... but the oomph to actually do it until now. I'm going to limit my social media participation for the month of July. Aside from all the crazy news cycles, I feel like social media has reduced my usage of other outlets such as this very blog, and other things like art and music.

For now I've only moved the social media apps on my phone, from the first page, to the second page; and I've also blocked twitter and facebook via hosts file entries on my computer ... just to maintain discipline.

Habits are difficult things to change. I hope to make a bit of a change in my habits.
